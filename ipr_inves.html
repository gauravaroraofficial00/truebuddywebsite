Case Study 1

Enforcement action on formulator of counterfeit pesticides in Maharashtra, India

Case:
The client company had suffered huge losses due to counterfeiting of one of their famous brand of pesticides in Maharashtra, Gujarat, U.P and Bihar.
  How did TBCPL help?
After months long investigation in Maharashtra, TBCPL team identified the formulator (also the main importer, formulator and supplier) of counterfeit pesticide in question, traced his formulating unit and facilitated enforcement action against him. The infringer was arrested, his formulating unit was shut down and a major problem of the client in that region was resolved.

Case Study 2

Enforcement action on processors of counterfeit seeds and supplier of parent seeds of a renowned agriculture client company in South India.

Case:
The infringers used to arrange parent seeds of the client company from organizers, multiply it in their fields. Provide these parent seeds to their empaneled farmers to grow the seeds illegally.
  How did TBCPL help?
Investigation and enforcement action was conducted in three phases.

  • Phase -1
    TBCPL conducted market survey in specified markets in South India, identified retailers of counterfeit seeds and conducted enforcement action against the infringers. Around 100 kg of counterfeit seeds seized.  
  • Phase -2
    After conducting enforcement action on retailers, TBCPL Team identified growers of illegal seed, put 24 hours surveillance on the fields and traced the mastermind and his processing unit. The team facilitated enforcement action on the processing unit and over 140 tons of counterfeit seeds (costing over INR 28 million /-) were seized.    
  • Phase -3
    After conducting enforcement action on the processing unit, team traced supplier of parent seeds (and their traced storage locations) and facilitated enforcement action against the infringers. Seized over 20 tons of parent seeds (worth over INR 200 million)  

Case Study 3

Enforcement action on Packager of counterfeit seeds in Western Uttar Pradesh, India

Case:
The Packager used to arrange hybrid seeds from a local seed processing unit and used to pack these seeds in counterfeit packing of leading agriculture companies.
  How did TBCPL help?
Starting point of Investigation was an address of a small seeds shop which was closed couple of years back.

  • TBCPL team identified owner of the closed shop.
  • Covert investigation on the subject revealed that he was operating from his residence.
  • TBCPL team located the stock of counterfeit products and facilitated enforcement agencies in conducting enforcement action on his residence.
  • Seized material (including the packed/loose material, packing material and packaging machines etc) worth over Rs 5 million /-

Case Study 4

Resolving a major problem of counterfeit pesticides in North India

Case:
One of the pesticide formulators and suppliers based in Delhi became a major problem for most of the agriculture companies.
  How did TBCPL help?
TBCPL team identified the subject’s associates in neighboring state of Haryana and with the help of state agriculture department, conducted two consecutive raids on the subject’s allies. Total six people (allies of the subjects) were arrested and sent to judicial custody.
TBCPL team put pressure through Director General of Agriculture, Haryana and based on the statements of people arrested during the raid, an action was conducted on the subject in Delhi forcing him to stop his business of counterfeit products. The action solved a huge problem of counterfeit pesticides in entire North India.

The market of counterfeit products is on a constant rise in India, and has surpassed over Rs 40,000 crore in the organized sector alone. TBCPL helps support its client companies by identifying their Intellectual Property Rights infringers, operating at different levels (retailers, wholesalers, packagers/manufacturers); via extensive, indepth & discreet market surveys across the country.

Once the infringers are identified, TBCPL team assists client companies in collecting relevant evidence for conducting appropriate legal action against the infringers.

TBCPL assists its clients in carrying out law enforcement action against the people/business entities involved in malicious activities. This activity involves presenting the case before enforcement agencies, supporting agencies in enforcement actions, filing complaints/report on behalf of the client companies and providing post action support.

TBCPL team through their partners, assists client companies in conducting criminal/civil action against the individuals/entities found infringing their intellectual property rights.
