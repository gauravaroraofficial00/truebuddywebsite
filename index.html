Case Study 1

Investigation for E-Commerce company

Case:
One of the client companies in the e-commerce space received several complaints from different states that a few of the miscreants were cheating the public by offering fake franchisees of a client company.
  How did TBCPL help?
Based on the information (contact number and email id) of the miscreant received from the complainant, TBCPL team conducted extensive online and field investigation and traced the offenders in West Bengal. Based on the findings of the TBCPL team, an FIR was registered in West Bengal against the infringer that helped the client company protect their brand name in the market.

Case Study 2

Enforcement action on supplier of counterfeit Pharmaceutical products in NCR

Case:
A drug dealer was offering counterfeit super-specialty drugs of one of the leading pharmaceutical company through online portals.
  How did TBCPL help?
TBCPL was engaged to investigate the case and collect information about the supplier; also assist the client in conducting enforcement action against the infringer. After rigorous research and investigation for two weeks, the TBCPL team located the infringer in NCR and conducted enforcement action through Food and Drug Administration department; seizing counterfeit drugs worth over 2 million Indian Rupees.

Case Study 3

Fraud investigation for F&B Company

Case:
One of the client companies of TBCPL has received certain allegations (through whistleblower) of unethical practices by one of the company employees.
  How did TBCPL help?
The client engaged TBCPL for conducting a covert investigation with the aim to verify the allegation and collect evidence supporting the facts. During the covert investigation, the TBCPL team confirmed that part allegations of the whistleblower were correct; also revealed few other malicious activities by the suspects. Details of all these activities and evidence collected during the investigation were shared with the client company.
Based on TBCPL report, the client company took corrective action that helped the company avoid huge monitory and reputational losses.

Case Study 4

Resolving a major problem of counterfeit pesticides in North India

Case:
One of the client companies of TBCPL was suffering huge monitory and reputation loss due to flooding of counterfeit fertilizers in North East India
  How did TBCPL help?
Based on information received from a client company, TBCPL conducted a market sweep in the specified area and identified multiple infringers in fertilizer markets in Assam. After completing the market survey, TBCPL team collected relevant pieces of evidence against the infringers and filed a complaint against them in the concerned S.P office. Based on TBCPL’s complaint, the police team conducted multiple raids in two fertilizer markets simultaneously and seized a huge amount of counterfeit product from different shops. The main source (packaging unit) of counterfeit fertilizer was also raided and seized by the police team. An FIR was registered against six individuals including the main supplier of counterfeit seeds in the area. The action was also published by leading local newspapers and media channels. After the above enforcement action by a police team, a team of agriculture officers have conducted multiple raids in the area and seized a huge quantity of illegal agricultural products.
