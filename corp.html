Integrity check is an effective way to gain insight into an individual's profile and to assess whether they have anything in their immediate background that may be a cause for concern. It includes:

  1. Address & identity verification.
  2. Litigation check including county court judgments & bankruptcy.
  3. Directorships check.
  4. Checks with sanction databases for involvement in/association with fraud, money laundering etc.
  5. News/media search.
  6. Checks with regulators & industry/professional bodies (if required).
  7. Searches & cross referencing of media sources in relation to past employers. This will assess any issues of concern in relation to previous employment.
